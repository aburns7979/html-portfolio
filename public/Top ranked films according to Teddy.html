Top ranked films according to Teddy

The most rewatchable films of all time

Passion of the Christ

The passion of the Christ is a film which depicts the events leading up to and including the crucifixion of Jesus Christ. This movie is powerful and emotionally charged. A true must see film for believers.

The Mummy Franchise

The Mummy franchise is a bynge worthy series of fictional films based loosely on historical figures from ancient Egypt. These movies combine exciting chases and fight scenes with just enough sappy romance to which offers the perfect material for a date night with your favorite person!

Me, Myself, & Irene

Me, Myself, & Irene is a classic comedy film starring Jim Carrey who plays a single father with a mulitple personality disorder who is looking for love and fighting for his life the entire way. Wildly funny, this film is perfect for a night of laughter.
